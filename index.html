Explore the Concept

App Concept

WhisperWorld reimagines how we experience our surroundings by delivering bite-sized, context-relevant audio facts through your AirPods as you move through the world. Using spatial audio technology, these facts appear to emanate from the physical direction of landmarks themselves, creating a seamless blend of the digital and physical worlds.

Unlike traditional audio guides that require active engagement and planning, WhisperWorld operates in the background, intelligently determining which nearby landmarks merit attention based on your interests, the landmark's significance, and your current context. The result is a delightful layer of discovery added to your everyday routes and explorations.

WhisperWorld embodies the principles of "calm technology" - enhancing your awareness without demanding your attention, allowing you to keep your eyes up and engaged with the world around you.

Architecture & Privacy

WhisperWorld employs a hybrid architecture with heavy emphasis on on-device processing for privacy. Precise GPS coordinates never leave your device. Battery efficiency is achieved through intelligent geofence batching and adaptive location accuracy.
